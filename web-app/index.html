Repurchases

Industry Overview

Fannie Mae reported 2,100 repurchases in Q3 2022. This is down from 2,138 in the previous quarter which was an all-time high except for Q1 2013.

Even when controlling for origination volume, repurchases have been trending upward. In Q3 2022, repurchases accounted for 0.026% of all loans originated over the last 8 quarters, up from 0.023% the previous quarter. Generally 90 - 95% of all repurchases occur within the first 8 months since origination.
